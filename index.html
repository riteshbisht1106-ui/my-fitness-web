One year ago, I started with a simple dream: to master bodyweight exercises and unlock advanced calisthenics skills. What began as basic push-ups transformed into an incredible journey of discipline, consistency, and pure determination. Every skill unlocked is proof that with the right approach and mindset, anything is possible.

Month 1-2

The Beginning

Started with basic push-ups, pull-ups, and dips. Built foundational strength and body awareness.

Month 3-4

First Milestone: Handstand

First time holding a handstand for 5 seconds! Worked on balance, shoulder strength, and confidence.

Month 5-6

Handstand Pushup Achievement

First handstand pushup! Combined vertical strength with body control. Huge confidence boost!

Month 7-8

Muscle Up Unlocked

The explosive muscle up! Required months of pull-up and dip progressions. Worth every rep!

Month 9-10

Front Lever Progress

Intense core and back training. Achieved tuck front lever, building towards full front lever.

Month 11-12

Straddle Planche Mastered

The ultimate achievement! Straddle planche hold. Proof of dedication and consistency.

Key Lessons

  • 💪 Consistency beats intensity - train smart, not just hard
  • 🧠 Patience is key - skills take time to develop
  • 📊 Progressive overload - increase difficulty gradually
  • 🤝 Community matters - training with others keeps you motivated
  • 🎯 Form over ego - perfect technique builds lasting strength
