# Fitness Website Roadmap - "Fitness With Ritesh"

## 🎯 Website Purpose
Personal portfolio + Course/Program selling platform for calisthenics enthusiast & content creator

---

## 📄 PAGES & SECTIONS

### 1. **HOME PAGE**
   - **Hero Section**: Eye-catching headline + featured calisthenics skill video/image
     - Tagline: "Master Calisthenics. Master Yourself."
     - CTA Button: "Explore Programs" / "Learn More"
   - **Quick Stats Section**:
     - 1 Year Journey
     - 5+ Advanced Skills Unlocked
     - Instagram Followers
     - YouTube Subscribers
   - **Featured Section**: 3 top skills showcase (with images/videos)
   - **Latest Content**: Recent YouTube/Instagram posts

### 2. **ABOUT ME / MY JOURNEY**
   - Timeline of progression (beginner → handstand → muscle up → front lever → straddle planche)
   - Before/After transformation (if applicable)
   - Why calisthenics? Personal story
   - Current focus/future goals
   - Social proof: Instagram & YouTube channels

### 3. **PROGRAMS/COURSES** (Income Generator)
   - Beginner Calisthenics Course (pushups → handstand prep)
   - Intermediate Course (handstand → muscle up progression)
   - Advanced Course (front lever, straddle planche, advanced skills)
   - Pricing tiers
   - What's included (videos, guides, progression plans)
   - Testimonials/Reviews

### 4. **GALLERY**
   - Grid of achievement images (your 7 fitness images)
   - Video embeds (YouTube shorts/clips)
   - Before/after photos
   - Skill demonstrations
   - Interactive: Filter by skill (handstand, muscle up, planche, etc.)

### 5. **BLOG/TIPS**
   - Calisthenics tutorials
   - Nutrition tips
   - Training methodology
   - Skills breakdown
   - Injury prevention

### 6. **SOCIAL LINKS & CONTACT**
   - Links to: Instagram, YouTube, Twitter/X
   - Contact form (inquiries)
   - Newsletter signup
   - Footer with all links

## 🎨 DESIGN STYLE & LAYOUT

### Color Scheme (Suggested):
- **Primary**: Dark (charcoal/black #1a1a1a) - motivating, modern
- **Accent**: Neon Green (#00ff00) or Orange (#ff6b35) - energy, fitness vibes
- **Text**: White/Light gray on dark background
- **Alternative**: Light mode with dark accent (if you prefer minimalist)

### Typography:
- **Headings**: Bold, modern font (Montserrat, Poppins, Oswald)
- **Body**: Clean, readable (Inter, Roboto)

### Layout Style:
- **Sections**: Full-width, stacked
- **Hero**: Large background image/video
- **Gallery**: 3-column grid (desktop), 1-2 column (mobile)
- **Cards**: Clean, with shadows/hover effects
- **Navigation**: Sticky navbar, smooth scrolling

### Key Design Features:
✅ Mobile-responsive (your followers use mobile)
✅ Video embeds (showcase YouTube content)
✅ Image galleries (showcase your photos)
✅ Smooth animations & scroll effects
✅ Dark theme (fits fitness/gym vibes)
✅ Fast loading (optimize images)
✅ Clear CTAs (Join Course, Watch Now, Follow)

## 🛠 TECH STACK (Recommended)

**Frontend:**
- **Next.js** (React) - fast, modern, good for content/portfolio
- **TailwindCSS** - easy dark theme, responsive design
- **Framer Motion** - smooth animations

**Backend (if needed):**
- Email service (Nodemailer for contact form)
- Course hosting (Teachable, Kajabi, or custom)

**Hosting:**
- Vercel (Next.js optimized)
- Netlify (alternative)
