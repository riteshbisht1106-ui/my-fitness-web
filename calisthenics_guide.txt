# 30-DAY CALISTHENICS TRANSFORMATION GUIDE
# By Ritesh Bisht

Welcome to your 30-day journey! This guide is designed to build foundational strength and prepare you for advanced skills.

## WEEK 1-2: THE FOUNDATION
Focus on form and consistency.
- Standard Push-ups: 3 sets of 10-15 reps
- Pull-ups (or Bodyweight Rows): 3 sets of 5-8 reps
- Bench Dips: 3 sets of 10 reps
- Plank: 3 sets of 45 seconds

## WEEK 3-4: BUILDING EXPLOSION
Increase intensity and add skill prep.
- Diamond Push-ups: 3 sets of 10 reps
- Explosive Pull-ups: 3 sets of 5 reps
- Crow Stand (Handstand Prep): 5 attempts (hold as long as possible)
- Hollow Body Hold: 3 sets of 30 seconds

## NUTRITION BASICS
- Stay Hydrated: 3-4 liters of water daily.
- Protein: 1.6g to 2g per kg of body weight.
- Sleep: 7-8 hours for muscle recovery.

## FORM CHECK TIPS
- Always keep your core engaged.
- Full range of motion is better than high reps with bad form.
- Record your sets to check your alignment.
